# Stock Cur8d — Swing Trade Reference Guide

## Entry Checklist (must check all before entering)

- [ ] **Score ≥ 65** (large cap) / ≥ 55 (small cap) — scanner filters this automatically
- [ ] **EMA stack aligned**: 9 EMA > 21 EMA > 50 EMA, all rising
- [ ] **Volume ratio ≥ 1.5x** — at least 50% above average volume on breakout day
- [ ] **RSI between 55–80** — momentum zone; above 80 = overextended, below 55 = weak
- [ ] **Price above VWAP** — institutional buyers are in control
- [ ] **9 EMA distance ≤ +2%** (green badge) — you're entering fresh, not chasing
- [ ] **R:R ≥ 1.5** — minimum reward vs risk before entry; prefer 2:1 or better
- [ ] **Clear catalyst** — news, earnings beat, sector strength, or breakout above key level

**Never enter when 9 EMA badge is red (+6% above) — you are chasing.**

## Stop-Loss Rules (non-negotiable)

1. **Set stop BEFORE entering** — copy the stop price from the Alert Levels section on the detail page
2. **Use Stop Market** (not Stop Limit) in Syfe Trade — guaranteed exit even on gap-down
3. **Stop placement**: Just below the 9 EMA or the base low (shown on the card and detail page)
4. **Never widen your stop** once placed — if it feels too tight, exit and reassess
5. **1-2% rule**: Never risk more than 1-2% of total capital on a single trade

### How to set a stop in Syfe Trade:
1. Buy the stock first (market or limit order at entry zone)
2. After fill: go to portfolio → tap the position → "Sell" → select "Stop Market"
3. Set stop price = the value shown in Alert Levels (Stop row)
4. Confirm — the order sits live until triggered or you cancel it

## EMA Cross (9/21) — What It Means

When the 9 EMA crosses above the 21 EMA ("Cross" badge on card):
- This signals the **start of a new momentum leg**, not necessarily a breakout
- Use it as **timing confirmation** when combined with a green EMA stack and RS > 70
- Best entries: pullback to 9 EMA after the cross, not the cross itself
- Failure: if price immediately drops back below both EMAs, exit quickly

---

## Partial Profit Taking

- At **+5-6%**: Move stop to breakeven (cost basis), take no profits yet
- At **+8-10%**: Sell 1/3 of position, let the rest run
- At **+15%** (or target): Sell remaining position or trail stop tightly below 9 EMA
- **Never** let a +10% winner turn into a loss — breakeven stop is mandatory

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Entering when 9 EMA distance is > +4%** — you are buying someone else's gains
2. **Missing the stop** — set it before you sleep; the market doesn't care about your schedule
3. **Holding through earnings** — unless intentional, exit before earnings announcements
4. **Averaging down** — if the stock hits your stop, you were wrong; don't double down
5. **Trading small caps with large-cap position sizes** — small caps can gap -20% overnight
6. **Chasing after a big up day** — wait for a pullback to the 9 EMA the next day

## Volume Histogram on Cards — How to Read It

The mini bar chart below the sparkline shows the last 20 days of volume:
- **Light grey bars** = past volume (reference)
- **Green last bar** = today's volume > 1.5x average (strong buying)
- **Dark grey last bar** = today's volume is normal or below average

A green last bar on a green price day = **institutional conviction**.

## SGT Trading Workflow

**Before 9:30 PM SGT (pre-market):**
- Check the scanner for fresh picks
- Review the detail page for each pick: chart, signals, trade setup
- Use the Claude deep analysis and chat to validate the thesis
- Copy alert levels (Stop, 9 EMA, Target) to your notes app

**9:30 PM – 10:30 PM SGT (first hour):**
- Best time to enter — highest volume, clearest direction
- Watch for volume confirmation on breakout
- Enter at the entry zone shown on the pick card; use limit orders

**10:30 PM – 1:30 AM SGT:**
- Monitor only; avoid new entries (lunch lull, lower volume)
- If the position is up, trail your stop up to just below current 9 EMA

**1:30 AM – 2:30 AM SGT (power hour):**
- Strong directional moves often happen here
- Good time to take partial profits if target is close

**After 2:30 AM SGT:**
- Review open positions before sleep
- Make sure stop-loss orders are live in Syfe Trade
- Do not hold speculative picks through the weekend without a confirmed stop

## Quick Reference: When to Hold vs Exit

| Situation | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| Up 10%+, stop at breakeven | Let it run to target; trail stop to 9 EMA |
| Up 5-8%, RSI approaching 80 | Sell 1/3, hold rest |
| Price dips to 9 EMA on low volume | Hold — normal pullback |
| Price closes below 9 EMA on volume | Exit — momentum broken |
| Volume dries up for 2+ days | Consider exiting — no follow-through |
| Macro event (Fed, CPI) approaching | Reduce position size before |
